# PM Plan Sprint Task

## Purpose
Plan the upcoming sprint by selecting tasks, estimating effort, and balancing workload.

## Steps
1. Review product backlog
2. Prioritize tasks based on business value
3. Estimate task complexity (story points)
4. Balance sprint capacity
5. Create sprint goals
6. Assign tasks to team members

## Considerations
- Team capacity and availability
- Technical dependencies
- Business priorities
- Risk factors
- Learning opportunities
